Example Phrases

! hi → Hello!

!- bye → Goodbye!

I=U → I am you

! U=+ → Hi, I agree with you

?U=s(hi) → Did you say hi?

I=k^ → I understand / Ok

I=L^ → I am laughing!

if(U=F) → Only if you are female

else(I=M) → Else I am male

How To Use kL

  • Combine symbols and operators to express thoughts concisely.
  • Use ? at the start for questions.
  • Chain statements with & (and), : (or), else() for alternatives.
  • Use variable assignment (=, ≠) for logic and conditions.
  • Add context with ~ for time, L^ for laughing, you can even define Your own variable(s) with "#"
