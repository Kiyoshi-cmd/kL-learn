kL – The KiyoLanguage

A constructed language by Kiyoshi

kL is a minimalist symbolic language for concise and creative communication.

About kL-learn

kL-learn is made for the Upizin community to have an origin language to themselves, for strategic communications. Think of it as a fast way to communicate between group(s) while the people around you may not understand the message.

"kL" used to be FCU (Fast Communication in Upizin), but as the group evolved, so did the language. It's now called kL, reflecting its creative and adaptive nature.

Why? The Upizin Group needed a way to chat in games using public chat, where known languages could be easily read by everyone. kL provides a layer of privacy, speed, and fun.

How To Use kL

  • Combine symbols and operators to express thoughts concisely.
  • Use ? at the start for questions.
  • Chain statements with & (and), : (or), else() for alternatives.
  • Use variable assignment (=, ≠) for logic and conditions.
  • Add context with ~ for time, L^ for laughing, you can even define your own variable(s) with "#".
  • Short forms save time in fast-paced chats or games.

kL Philosophy

kL is about efficiency, creativity, and connection within the Upizin community and beyond. It's a symbol-based language designed to be simple to learn but fun to master.

  • Minimalist: Only the most necessary symbols and structures.
  • Group Identity: Fosters a unique bond and fast communication.
  • Strategic: Useful for games, teamwork, and anywhere you want to chat privately in public!

Origin Story (from README)

The Upizin Group was founded in early 2023. During gameplay, group members needed a way to coordinate without being easily understood in public chat. Thus, kL was born—simple, fast, and flexible!

Over time, the language grew with the group, adding new operators, logic, and even jokes. It's a living language, and everyone is encouraged to help it evolve.
